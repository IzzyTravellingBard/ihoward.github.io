The King of Cups

Isabel writes and performs original music under the name The King of Cups. They have recorded tracks at Ailfionn Recording Studio in Dublin, Ireland and played numerous sessions at Walshes Pub Stoneybatter with members of Twin Headed Wolf, Branwen, Varo and Ye Vagabonds.

Dublin Digital Radio

Isabel hosts The King of Cups Radio, a transatlantic radio show appearing bi-weekly from 3-4 Irish time (10-11 East Coast time) on Dublin Digital Radio. The King of Cups Radio features storytelling and song that has influenced the artist's music and performance. It is The King of Cups spirit broadcast on air.
