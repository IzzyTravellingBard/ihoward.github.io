I.Howard

Isabel Howard is a writer of poetry and prose, a musician, photographer, web developer and scholar. Their interests include folk, country and blues music, medieval art & literature, film photography, 20th century American literature, and Digital Humanities. They earned a First Class Honours BA from Trinity College Dublin in Dublin, Ireland. They currently reside in Virginia, USA.
